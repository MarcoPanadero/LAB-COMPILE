Jujutsu Kaisen

Jujutsu Kaisen is an Anime about a boy named Yuji Itadori who wants to seek what he calls a "proper death". After his grandfathers death he becomes a Jujutsu Sorcerer by eating "Sukuna's Finger" which gave him his cursed energy. Now with the help of The Strongest, Satoru Gojo, and Fushiguro Megumi, a student of Jujutsu High, he attends Jujutsu high to fulfill his goal.
